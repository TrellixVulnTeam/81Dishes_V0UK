Welcome to 81 Dishes

81 Dishes is a well maintained family restaurant. It has cuisines like Indian, Arabic, Chineese and Malasian. It is located near Chavakkad beach. The balcony view which we provides gives a good experiance to the customers. We have a our own style of dishes which makes us famous.

81 Dishes is where memories are created, tales shared and moments captured. Enjoy a picture-perfect cuisine experience in an ambience that narrates the tastiest tales.

Write on the golden sands of Chavakkad beach a tale of the perfect bite of an even perfect food with 81 Dishes. Sit close to the sparkling warm sands and stay grounded to experience tasty tales that live happily forever.
